About Macon County

Discover our history and leadership structure

History

Macon County was established in 1828 and is located in the western region of North Carolina. Known for its natural beauty, historical roots, and growing community, Macon County is a vibrant place to live, work, and visit.

Government Structure

The County operates under a Commissioner-Manager form of government. Elected commissioners oversee legislation and policy, while a county manager implements day-to-day operations.
